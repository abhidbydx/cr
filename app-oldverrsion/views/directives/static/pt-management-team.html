Management Team

Dhruv Agarwala, Co-founder

Dhruv brings along with him several years of experience. Before PropTiger came into being, Dhruv was the CEO of GE's Infrastructure business in India, and also led Institutional Sales for GE in the country. He has previously held various senior roles with GE in the US and has world-class operating and general management experience.

Dhruv had previously started a highly successful iron-ore mining company in India, and he also started and exited an education business offering management training. Dhruv holds a B.S. in Materials Science from Northwestern University, an M.S. from Stanford University and an MBA from Harvard.

Kartik Varma, Co-founder

Kartik has an extensive background of financial advisory and investment management experience in India, Asia,US and Europe. He invested money for the Ziff family and thereafter for Europe's leading hedge fund, TCI Fund Management, which managed money for wealthy families, institutions and endowments. Before his entry into investing, Kartik worked as a mergers and acquisitions banker at James D. Wolfensohn, Inc. in New York.

Kartik also managed the operations for India's largest theatre company, Stagedoor, and helped grow the troupe into having a pan-India presence. He holds a Bachelor in Arts from Delhi University, an LL.B. from the London School of Economics and an MBA from Harvard. During his business school days, he also worked on starting a Formula1 racing team.

Prashan Agarwal, Co-founder

Prashan brings along a varied experience of over 10 years in Business Strategy, Operations and IT. He also possesses in-depth knowledge of the real estate industry in India.

Prior to PropTiger, Prashan initiated a leading internet real estate brokerage business in India. He has worked on senior roles with GE, ITC and Sapient. He holds a Bachelor of Technology from IIT Kanpur and an MBA from Indian School of Business Hyderabad.
